Welcome

This website provides basic information about famous Indian sports leagues like IPL (cricket), ISL (football), and PKL (kabaddi).

Main Leagues

IPL (Indian Premier League)

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India, widely considered the most popular and richest cricket league in the world. It features ten city-based franchise teams competing annually between March and May.

ISL (Indian Super League)

The Indian Super League (ISL) is the top-tier professional football league in India, founded in 2013 with the goal of making football more popular in the country.

PKL (Pro Kabaddi League)

The Pro Kabaddi League (PKL) is a professional men's kabaddi league in India, widely recognized as the most popular and second most-watched sports league in India after the Indian Premier League (IPL). It has transformed the traditional Indian rural sport into a modern, high-energy spectacle..
