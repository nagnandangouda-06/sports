Important Non-Cricket Leagues in India

Apart from cricket, India also has leagues for other sports such as badminton, hockey and volleyball. These leagues help promote different sports among youth and improve international performance.

League Name Sport Started Type
Premier Badminton League (PBL) Badminton 2016 Franchise Based
Hockey India League (HIL) Hockey 2013 Franchise Based
Pro Volleyball League Volleyball 2019 Franchise Based
Ultimate Table Tennis (UTT) Table Tennis 2017 Franchise Based

Benefits of Indian Sports Leagues

  • Encourage young talent in different sports.
  • Increase viewership and fan interest.
  • Improve national and international competition levels.
  • Provide better career opportunities for athletes.
